What is ICT

Information and communications technology (ICT) is often used as an extended synonym for information technology (IT), but is a more specific term that stresses the role of unified communications. ICT provides access to information through telecommunications. It is similar to Information Technology (IT), but concentrates mostly on communication technologies such as the Internet, wireless networks, cell phones, and other communication mediums. ICT includes any communication device or application, encompassing: radio, television, cell phones, computer, laptops, tablets, and network hardware and software, so on. ICT also provides the various services online and applications associated with them[1]

Social Impacts
Social networking

With the development of communication technologies, the reach of communication has extended to a global level.[2] This has it's effect on many different areas, most notably, social networking. The communication that can now be had between people that may be in entirely different parts of the world have allowed for a society that is more connected than ever before. The effects of this are seen in modern political movements. The sharing of ideas to the world has allowed for fast developments in humanitarian issues that previously would have a hard time going through. The power of social networking is seen everywhere and is a major contributor to our development as a society

Information sharing

Access to information has increased drastically with the development of communication of communication tech like the internet. The effects of this are more visible in the education system. Over the years, the content taught has been progressively shifted backwards teaching the younger generations harder and harder things. This is due to the simple fact that the information is constantly readily available to anyone exposing people at young ages to things their parents hadn't learned until they were older. Another place it can be seen is in the exponentially increasing speed of development of science. With the ability to share data, more people access to the information at once increasing the chance of someone being able to build on it and improve it. The result of this were drastic technological and scientific advancements made in a very short span of time.

Employment
Job opportunities

The existence of communications technologies has opened up the possibility for millions of jobs. Network administrators, Web developers, Certification companies, Browser companies, and many more jobs who's purpose is to build and manage communication tech. This opens up a lot of room for both employment and also self-employment. People can create their own business shops without the heavy overheads of place, purchase items and sales in market and make the advertising campaigns use of multimedia application. This increased sales and encourages the small businesses.[2]
